Background
Sarah Sutho birth name is Siti Maisarah Ma'arof and was born Thursday of 4th March 1999. She is a Malay(Jawa-Banjar) by ethnicity. She was actively joining Taekwondo and Athletic Fields as well as actually got through the scholl team to compete in a few of big important sports avenue among MJSC's college.

Sarah was also a Science-Art student and has a good reputation of being the 'Artist' of the college. She has also joined few Arts Fair and her painting was one of the chosen one to get pinned in the MARA Student's logbook.
Sarah has also opened commision for drawing at most twice. Since the course she is pursuing later in her college life is computer science related,she drop practises and her artistic skills eventually.

She is the eldest among three of her sisters and love foods.
Musics
Sarah Sutho is a Soul-RnB singer based on her singing style. Despite having zero musical background since she young. Despite the obvious artistic talents(singing annd drawing) spotted by her mom, however,the family ignore the singing talent and proceeds with the drawing. However, both of her parents has naturally gifted voices and basic understanding regarding music. It has been told that music would not given any benefits and has 'no future' for her.

She discovers music only when she enters high education line as she begin to participate in few singing compettition. Later in her first year of degree, she has been actively joining bands and attending few performing invitation to wedding and festive ceremonies.

The following in late 2019, when she found Andy and Azad and created the band Cherry Lona. Andy then contacted Nik, his former guitarist from the The Rudean. Not long after that, Covid-19 hit the nation and the 2years were the period for her to improve her musical sense as what she is now.

Sarah is influenced heavily by so-called Black music which often associated to Gospel and Soul church style. She looks up to almost all singers who fights and bravily continue spreading Soul-RnB-Jazz musics.
